00 - Pilot - Impostor Syndrome

32:46 | 01 Sep 2015 | MP3 or OGG

Software development is a fast-paced, continually changing field. Whether you've just starting learning how to program or you've been doing it for 30 years, at some point in your career you'll feel like you don't belong. This is inevitable in any field where the job requires you to always be learning and keeping up with the latest trends.

As times passes and your career progresses, impostor syndrome may manifest itself differently. What are the benefits and downsides to impostor syndrome? How can you use it to drive your career forward instead of holding you back?

Writing software isn't just a difficult technical task, at some point it will be difficult emotionally as well. At Grok we've all experienced this. Listen to how some of us handle impostor syndrome, including our most experienced team members.
