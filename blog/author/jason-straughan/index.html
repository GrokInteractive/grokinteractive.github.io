ABOUT THE AUTHOR
Jason Straughan

The CEO of Grok Interactive, Jason is a long-time programmer, mentor, and teacher who has worked with all manner of proven talent in Ruby on Rails, PHP, javascript, HTML, CSS, and most major databases.

He is the author of "Beginning Kohana 3.0 Development" for Packt Publishing, co-founder of Codeup.com, and organizer of the San Antonio Web Development Meetup.
