Express.js

A not so small introduction to 3 parts of the MEAN stack

  John Guest     Oct 02, 2014    Training Node.js, Mongodb, Javascript, Socket.io, Express.js

Node.js is becoming increasingly more popular in modern web development. Here is a small tutorial intended to walk someone with little to no experience in full stack javascript development through three parts of the MEAN stack -- MongoDB/Mongoose, Express.js and Node.js -- plus a short Socket.io introduction. The tutorial uses a small comment feature to show a reader how parts of the stack come together.
