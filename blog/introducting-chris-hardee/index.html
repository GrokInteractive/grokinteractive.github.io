Grok Welcomes Chris Hardee to the Team

by Jason Straughan    Nov 11, 2013    General Grok

We are pleased to announce that Chris Hardee is joining the Grok Interactive team.

Chris is a cofounder and CEO of 10BitWorks.com, also known as the San Antonio Hackerspace. Helping grow the tech and hacker communities in San Antonio is an important mission, and we are very excited to have Chris on our team.

Chris has experience with C++, PHP including ExpressionEngine and Wordpress, and Ruby on Rails. His front-end chops are strong; CSS and HTML are putty in the hands of this web sorcerer.

We knew we liked Chris when he fired up Emacs and quickly defended his opinions. Any geek that will wrestle that IDE while building 3D printers on the weekends has a place on our team.

Welcome to Grok, Chris. We hope you enjoy your stay.

ABOUT THE AUTHOR
Jason Straughan

The CEO of Grok Interactive, Jason is a long-time programmer, mentor, and teacher who has worked with all manner of proven talent in web and mobile application development.

He is the author of "Beginning Kohana 3.0 Development" for Packt Publishing, co-founder of Codeup.com, and organizer of the San Antonio Web Developer Meetup.
